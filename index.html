Lets play Whack-a-mole!

Game instructions:

30 moles will pop up on a board containing of 9 holes (3x3)
Click (and whack) the mole when it pops up. 1 point per whack!
They are clever creatures so they will pop up in a rateing speed to avoid the whacks
But be careful, the mole keeps track of your strikes (missed whacks)
